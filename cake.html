San Sebastian Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g cream cheese
  • 200g heavy cream
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 15g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Grease and line a round baking pan.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the cream cheese and sugar. Mix until smooth.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and mix until the batter is well combined.
  6. Gently fold in the flour, vanilla extract, and salt until just incorporated.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cheesecake cool to room temperature.
  10. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.

Serving:

Slice and serve the San Sebastian Cheesecake as is or with a dusting of powdered sugar.

Enjoy your delicious San Sebastian Cheesecake!
